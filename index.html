Vision

Picture a healthier world..

Building a fairer and healthier world by taking health equity much more seriously than before. – and meet head-on the social and economic.

Providing high-quality, cost-effective health care services to residents of the greater Pomona Valley. The Medical Center offers a full range of services from local primary acute care to highly specialized regional services. Selection of all services is based on community need, availability of financing and the organization‘s technical ability to provide high quality results. Basic to our mission is our commitment to strive continuously to improve the status of health by reaching out and serving the needs of our diverse ethnic, religious and cultural community.
